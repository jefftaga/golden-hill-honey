About Golden Hill Honey

Pure. Natural. Golden. From our family hives to your table.

At Golden Hill Honey, we believe every drop of our honey carries out the essence of our island.

Our Story

Golden Hill Honey is a family owned business thats based in Saipan. We began with a simple mission: to bring natural, organic honey to our community while protecting the environment and supporting sustainable beekeeping practices as well as to share sweetness that is as natural as the land it came from!

Our Mission

We are dedicated to practice ethical beekeeping, ensuring the health/welfare of our bees, and producing honey that reflects the purity and natural touch of nature. Every jar is harvested with care and respect for the land from once it came.

  • Ethical beekeeping practices
  • Healthy thriving bees
  • Honey that reflects nature's purity

Sustainability

Our bees thrive in carefully maintained environments. We avoid harmful chemicals and pesticides. Our focus on practices that preserve biodiversity are of utmost importance. Sustainability is at the heart of everything we do at Golden Hill Honey.
